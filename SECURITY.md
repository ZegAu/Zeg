# Security policy

Thank you for helping keep Zeg secure.

Reporting a vulnerability
- Preferred: Send an encrypted email to security@zeg.com.au with full details.
- Alternative: Open a private repository security advisory or a private issue (if available).

What to include
- Description of the issue and impact.
- Steps to reproduce (ideally a minimal repro).
- A suggested fix, if possible.
- Affected versions.

Encryption
- We publish our PGP key on request. If you need it, email support@zeg.com.au and we will supply the key fingerprint.

Response targets
- We acknowledge receipt within 48 hours.
- For critical issues we aim to publish a fix or mitigation within 7 days; timelines for lower severities vary by impact.
- We will coordinate disclosure and crediting of researchers unless otherwise requested.

Disclosure
- Please do not publicly disclose the vulnerability before we have provided a fix or a mitigation plan.
